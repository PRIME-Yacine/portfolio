A2 · Approach

From confirmation calls to clean dashboards

I'm a data analyst based in Algiers, Algeria, focused on business intelligence for e-commerce operations. My work sits where logistics meets revenue — cash-on-delivery confirmation rates, return analysis, delivery-agent performance and ad spend ROI: the numbers that decide whether a direct-to-consumer brand's unit economics actually hold up.

I came up through Excel and Google Sheets before moving into Power BI, SQL and Python, and most of my first drafts still start in a spreadsheet — that's where operations teams actually work. Every dashboard I ship is built to be read by the person running the warehouse floor, not just the person presenting the boardroom slide.
